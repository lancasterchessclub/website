Club Champion - Nikodem Wolnik

27th July 2022

Congratulations to our new Club Champion - Nikodem Wolnik. In a year which has seen us move to the Bridge club and an influx of new members the club championship proved to be fairly elusive. Lawrence proposed various formats and eventually settled on a knockout format which was played out early in May.

After beating Keerthi in the semi-finals, Niko overcame another hugely strong junior player Ewan in the final as white to be crowned the Club Champion for the 2021/2022 season, a worthy successor of Barry.
