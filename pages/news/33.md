# Lancaster, Morecambe & District Chess League Prizegiving

The annual prizegiving for the Lancaster, Maorecambe and District chess league was held at Lancaster Chess Club on Tuesday 19/5/2026.

The 2025/26 winners were:

**Division One Champions** – Lancaster ‘A’ (captain James McKiernan)

**Division Two Champions** – University 'B' (captain George Bland) 

**Lansil Cup** – Bare Village 'A' (captain Phil Seery) 

**Nolan Cup** – Kendal (captain Alex Billings) 

**Quick-Play** – Bare Village ‘A’ (captain Phil Seery) 

**Level Champion** – Sam Barlow (Dragons) 

**Handicap Champion** – Jack Davies (Dragons) 

**Under 110 (1525) Champion** – Jonathan Verden (Dragons) 

**Player of the Year** – Om Reddy Jayar (Lancaster 'A') & Charlie Warboys (University B')

**Jamboree** winners – Lancaster 'A'
