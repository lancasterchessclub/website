# **Max's first simultaneous display in Cleveley**

On Thursday night our club prodigy Max Fyson took on 26 boards at Cleveleys Junior Chess Club playing his first ever simultaneous.

Max put in an impressive performance winning 25 and losing one (25-1) against a strong cohort of Cleveley youngsters.

With Max’s rating increasingly rapidly towards 2150 he continues to progress immensely. Well done Max!
