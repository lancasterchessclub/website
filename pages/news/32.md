**5th Lancaster Rapidplay Congress - 24th January 2026**

Another Congress been and gone! Thank you so much to everyone who played and supported our event. We hope to see you all for our standard play in August! (Details will be on website soon)

This rapidplay we had some great competitive chess across the boards in all 4 sections. Along with the usual prize winners we had diversity prizes for, best female player, best player 65 and over and best u16 player.
There are pictures below taken by club photographer and social media manager Harrison Theobald. More pictures to follow this post.
